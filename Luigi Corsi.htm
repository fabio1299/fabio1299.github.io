A proposal for an Oil Industry Museum

The reasons behind this idea

At the end of the Fifties, when I was a young petroleum engineer, environmental problems were not a concern. I believe that over the next twenty years hydrocarbons will decline in importance as energy sources, gradually replaced by renewable and non-polluting alternatives.

All the same, the past century-and-a-half of greatly improved living conditions -- fueled largely by oil -- cannot be forgotten. This is why humankind today needs to preserve the evidence of the oil industry's achievements.

This is why the idea of an interactive museum of the oil industry must come to fruition.
